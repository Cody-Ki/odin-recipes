Meatloaf

This is an amazing meatloaf from my girlfriends family that made me a fan of meatloaf

  • Serves: 4-5
  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 2lbs of ground beef
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 10 saltine crackers (crumbled)
  • 1 tsp of worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 tsp of pepper
  • 1 can of vegetable soup

Cooking Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients and mix
  2. Put into greased loaf pan
  3. Bake at 350F for one hour
  4. Drain excess juices from baking then serve
