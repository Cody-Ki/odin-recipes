Crack Chicken

This dish is an easy slow cooker recipe that is delicious

  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Cook Time: 30 minutes (can rest in crock pot)

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless/skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 8oz packs of cream cheese
  • 1 package of real bacon bits
  • 2 packages of ranch dip mix
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook chicken until tender
  2. Shred chicken and put into crockpot with rest of ingredients
  3. Let sit in crock pot on low until everything is melted together
  4. Serve on buns and enjoy
